Summer internship

Social Ape Marketing is a forward-thinking marketing company that produces creative content for clients to build brand awareness and produce results one engaging conversation at a time. We seek to hire a talented intern who has the desire to learn and work in a high paced, fun environment. Our ideal candidate is enthusiastic, passionate about social media and public relations, and dedicated to growing Social Ape.
The internship will begin: As soon as possible.

Responsibilities

  • Create social media content for Social Ape Marketing
  • Attend marketing events and client events with the Social Ape
  • Administrative duties
  • Update the Social Ape Marketing website as needed
  • Create graphic design content
  • Work with the Social Ape team to plan and execute
  • Help create the Social Ape weekly newsletter
  • Design and order promotional products
  • Update and publish Google Adwords

Requirements

  • Proficient with all major social media platforms
  • 15 hours per week in the Social Ape office
  • An outgoing personality with the ability to reach out to media
  • Ability to think outside the box and have a creative mindset
  • Basic photo editing and graphic design skills
  • Experience with Microsoft Office and Google products
  • Flexible schedule with ability to work nights and weekends
  • Positive attitude
  • An insatiable drive to grow and learn new concept
